Mashed potatoes

Description

This is a quick and easy mashed potatoes recipe that can be easily veganized.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes cubed
  • 1/2 container (8 oz) cream cheese
  • 1/2 container (12 oz) sour cream
  • 1/4 Cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Place potatoes in a large pot of lightly salted water. Bring to a boil, and cook until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain, and mash.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mashed potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream, milk, onion salt, and pepper. Transfer to a large casserole dish.
  3. Place in crockpot for serving later or serve immediately.
